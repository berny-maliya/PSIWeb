where we are

METALWORKS

We supply a number of metalworks customers with specialized lubricants. Cuting, molding, stamping and many more complex processes are executed using our products.

TRANSPORTATION

Our oils, greases and specialized motor fluids for gasoline and diesel engines comply with most OEM's specifications. All our engine lubricating products are API certified.

ENERGY

We supply PEMEX and CFE in México. We have custom developed high technology products used in drilling, processing and distributing oil derivatives and energy obtained by various means.

MINING

Our multipurpose, hydraulic and high specialty oils are used in mines owned and operated by such important industrial groups as Grupo México and Minera Frisco.

SUGAR INDUSTRY

We are the main supplier of very high tech and specialty lubricants to the sugar industry. Our products are NSF and FDA certified.

STEEL INDUSTRY

We have developed highly specialized solutions for the demanding needs of the steel industry. From world class synthetic hydraulic oils, to fire resistant fluids.

AUTOMOTIVE

.We offer a full lineup of automotive lubricants & fluids. Our API certified motor oils cover all the driving, operation and temperature ranges present in Mexico. We are one or the biggest suppliers of private labels in retail.

ROBOTICS

We develop and manufacture a number of lubricants, both oils and greases, used in specialized equipment operating at high speeds and low pressure in controled environments.
